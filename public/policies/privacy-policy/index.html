Why Baby Cry - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 19, 2026 Last Updated: January 19, 2026

We Don’t Collect Your Data

Why Baby Cry processes everything on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers.

What Data Stays on Your Device

Audio Recordings

  • Purpose: Analyze baby cry patterns
  • Storage: Temporarily stored in device memory during analysis
  • Deletion: Automatically deleted immediately after analysis
  • Transmission: Never uploaded to servers or third parties

Analysis Results

  • Storage: Local device only (using SwiftData)
  • Content: Cry type classification, confidence scores, timestamps, user feedback
  • Does Not Include: Original audio recordings, personal identifiable information
  • Your Control: Delete individual records or clear all history in Settings

Adaptive Learning

  • How It Works: Adjusts predictions based on your feedback locally
  • Storage: Learning data remains on your device only
  • Privacy: Never shared with other users or our servers

Device Synchronization (Optional)

  • iPhone ↔ Apple Watch: Sync analysis results via Apple Watch Connectivity framework
  • Encryption: Data transferred through Apple’s encrypted channels
  • No External Servers: Data never passes through our servers
  • Your Control: Can be disabled in Settings

Permissions Required

Microphone Access

  • Purpose: Record baby cries for analysis
  • When Requested: First time you start recording
  • If Denied: App cannot perform cry analysis
  • Manage: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone → Why Baby Cry

Third-Party Services

Why Baby Cry does NOT use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Data collection tools
  • Cloud processing services

All machine learning inference runs locally using Apple CoreML.

Children’s Privacy

This app is designed to help parents care for their babies. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

This app complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Data Security

  • All data stored in your device’s secure sandbox environment
  • Protected by iOS system-level encryption
  • Only you can access your data
  • All local data permanently deleted when app is uninstalled

Your Rights

Access

  • View all analysis records in the History tab

Delete

  • Delete individual records
  • Clear all history in Settings
  • Uninstall app to remove all data

Control

  • Disable adaptive learning
  • Disable device synchronization
  • Revoke microphone access

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy:

  • “Last Updated” date will be updated
  • You will be notified in-app for significant changes
  • Continued use of the app indicates acceptance
